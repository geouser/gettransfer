In this section you will find answers to the most frequently asked questions.

What kind of information do we collect and use?

In order to validate a booking we need some personal information about you such as name of person who is booking a car, address, phone number, e-mail and credit card details. We also need details about trip or flight and place of accommodation. This information is necessary to deliver a car to appropriate airport or station at the appointed time and to know at which hotel to deliver you. In case of phone number and e-mail address, it is necessary for sending you a booking voucher and contacting you before your trip. Your address is required to process transaction using your credit card and authenticate in order to prevent any kind of possible fraud.

Who can get the data about clients?

The company Gettransfer is collecting information only with the purpose of processing your booking. If you have made a booking through the main Gettransfer.az website, you can be sure that we will not turn over, sell or place at somebody else’s disposal your personal information.

Data security and protection

Our company follows the stringent safety rules of information storage and disclosure so as to prevent any illegal access. In accordance with our security procedures, we may occasionally request proof of identity before disclosing confidential information.

Cookie files

Cookies are small files that is saved by your browser and placed on your computer’s hard disk. Our cookies do not contain any person identifying information. Cookies can be removed from your computer’s hard disk. The most part of browsers save Cookies automatically but you can usually change the browser settings to prevent it. Even without Cookies you can gain access to the most majority of our functions.

Luggage

PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS MAY BE SUBJECT TO A FURTHER CHARGE FOR WHICH YOU WILL BE LIABLE AT THE TIME. Please inform us at the time of booking by telling our booking agent if you wish to travel with excess baggage including, but not limited to, surfboards, bicycles, pet carriers or other oversized items. This is to ensure that our vehicles can accommodate you. Should you fail to notify us at the time of booking, you will be liable to us or our suppliers for any additional costs incurred in the carriage of such items.
