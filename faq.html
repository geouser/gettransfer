How to order a transfer?

After you have selected vehicle class (by pressing “select” in results list), you will be asked to fill out the booking form. First off all you will need to provide flight number and hotel name of your destination. If you don’t know flight number, type in your departure city (last one in case of a transit flight). If you are travelling from the airport, use your arrival time as a pick up. In case of a trip to airport, calculate pick up time using this formula: 2-3 hours before departure plus trip time.

Next step is to enter lead passenger contact information. Enter first and last name (will be used on a welcome sign), contact Email and phone number (including country code). Voucher and confirmation number will be sent to the email provided in your booking. Make sure your phone is switch on and can receive calls in roaming upon pick up so that driver can contact you in case of an emergency.
